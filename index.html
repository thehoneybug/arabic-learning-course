Why Learn Arabic?

📋

Let Quran reach you the way it was revealed

Translation is a viel. Allah's narration has an effect that no translation can have. Quranic words are the words chosen by Allah and what is more beatuiful than listening to these words as they were narrated.

🎯

Needs Consistence

Upto 30 minutes daily. Discipline is important.

📈

Small Steps are enough

If you learn one sentence per day; in a year you will know 365. If you don't do anything, you will be the same in 10 years as well.

🌟

Results will come (In Sha Allah)

In a year, bi-izni-Allah, people see results. You will as well in sha Allah.
